# Teleportation Math

Let:

```text
|psi> = alpha|0> + beta|1>
|Phi+> = (|00> + |11>) / sqrt(2)
```

The combined state can be rewritten in the Bell basis:

```text
|psi>|Phi+> = 1/2[
|Phi+>(|psi>) +
|Phi->(Z|psi>) +
|Psi+>(X|psi>) +
|Psi->(XZ|psi>)
]
```

The measurement outcome selects the correction needed on the receiver's qubit.
